# MB Storage - Facebook / Instagram content calendar

12 ready-to-post updates - roughly one a week for three months. Post to your
MB Storage Page, then cross-post the group-friendly ones into local Facebook
groups (Batley, Birstall, Liversedge, Cleckheaton, Heckmondwike, Brighouse,
Spen Valley community/selling groups).

**Every post drives to the same two actions:** get an instant quote at
mbstorage.co.uk or call 07375 355233. No "DM us", no "comment below".

**Group etiquette (so you don't get removed):** check each group allows
business posts (many have a specific day, e.g. "Small Business Sunday"). Keep
it friendly and local, not salesy. One group post a week per group is plenty.
Never post the same thing to ten groups in one minute - space them out.

**Image tip:** use your real yard photos and the container graphics from the
website. Square (1080x1080) works best for both Facebook and Instagram.
